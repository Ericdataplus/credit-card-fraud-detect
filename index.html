Key Findings

01

Data Augmentation = +3.3% Boost

Adding external 2023 fraud data increased training fraud rate from 0.17% to 35.75%, dramatically improving detection.

02

XGBoost Beats Deep Learning

Gradient boosting (91%) outperformed neural networks (75%) on this structured tabular dataset.

03

No Data Leakage Verified

Rigorous integrity check confirmed 0 duplicates between augmented data and test set.

04

Beats Academic Baselines

Our 88.49% (original data) exceeds typical research benchmarks of 85-86% for this dataset.

05

Feature Engineering Matters

Log-transforming amounts and creating V14*V17 interaction improved performance significantly.
